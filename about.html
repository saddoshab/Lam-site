About LAM

Little and More is a South Sudanese NGO working to protect children, improve education, health, and livelihoods in vulnerable communities.

Our Mission

To protect children and empower communities through health, education, and livelihoods.

Our Vision

A South Sudan where every child is safe, healthy, and educated.

Our Values

Integrity • Compassion • Community • Excellence

Our Story

Little and More (LAM) is a South Sudanese NGO founded in 2018 with one simple belief: small actions create big change.

We started by working in one community in Juba, focusing on child protection and basic health. Today, we work across 25+ communities to protect children, improve education, strengthen health systems, and build sustainable livelihoods.

At LAM, we don't just deliver aid. We work with communities to build solutions that last.
